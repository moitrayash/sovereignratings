§ Citations

All sources cited in the Methodology, Glossary, Stories, and supplementary pages. Entries follow the Chicago Manual of Style, 17th edition, author–date variant. Where a PDF is available offline, a VIEW PDF button opens the document in a new tab. Sources are grouped by type and sorted alphabetically by author within each group.

Citation conventions used here. First citation in any page gives author full names and year; subsequent citations within the same page use last names only. Multi-author papers use "and" (not "&") between the final two authors. Working papers cite the institutional series and number; journal articles cite volume, issue, and page range; reports cite the publishing body and year. URLs and DOIs are appended where they exist.
