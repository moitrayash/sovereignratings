## Entry 22 — 18:14 EDT — sidequest #8 finds the most important number of the night

I just shipped `paper_gini_popweighted.csv`. World-group Gini, pop-weighted vs unweighted, 2000–2025.

## Entry 29 — 18:31 EDT — the v63 hotfix that exposed me
